
name: PKPD influence of digoxin on Hemodynamics of Cardiovascular system

# Task

What is hemodynamic consequences on pulsatile pressure and volume characteristics after intravenous application of digoxin 0.5 mg

# Methods

Combined model of PKPD of digoxin and hemodynamics of cardiovascular system. Integrated 2 compartment model of pharmacokinetisc of digoxin with sigmoid emax effect of digoxin on effector site with reported coefficient ratio dela ke0 and it's influence on heart rate and variable elasticity during cardiac cycle. 

# Results
Visible small increase in systolic pressure, decrease in diastolic pressure and reduced heart rate shifts P-V volume to the right. Therapeutic target for atrial fibrilation.
